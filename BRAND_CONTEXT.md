## Landing Page Content Requirements
The landing page must present Swapnow as a healthy food brand focused on personalization and outcomes.

Required sections:
- Hero section for healthy food + personalized meal positioning
- Personalized meals / custom suggestion section (based on BMI)
- BMI calculation section
- Calorie intake calculation section
- Doctor consultation section
- Testimonials section
- Instagram reels video section

## Product Messaging Priorities
- Healthy lifestyle support
- Personalization using user health metrics
- Practical nutrition guidance
- Trust and credibility through expert consultation and customer testimonials

## Implementation Notes
- React components for plug-and-play integration will be provided separately.
- Avoid emojis across all website copy and UI content.
- Keep tone clean, modern, and health-focused.
